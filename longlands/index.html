The Masterplan

Visionary Design
& Smart Densification

The Masterplan is a collaborative achievement between renowned Gated Community Designers from Cape Town and Zimbabwe's leading town planners — approved by Chitungwiza Municipality in 2023.

Designed under Section 43 of the Regional Town and Country Planning Act Chapter 29:12, the estate integrates smart densification policies with luxury community living.

Why Invest
in Longlands?

Proven Market Performance
Gated communities command higher prices per m². Graylands Park launched at US$40/m² and now trades at US$72/m² — an 80% appreciation underpinning the sector's strength.
Economies of Scale
Nissam supplies its own quarry products, sand, bricks, and concrete blocks — dramatically reducing costs and passing premium value directly to investors and homeowners.
Asset-Backed Bond Innovation
Nissam is pioneering a tradeable Asset-Backed Bond secured against receivables, likely in partnership with a listed REIT — offering stability, liquidity, and currency risk mitigation.
Diaspora Target Market
Designed to appeal to the Zimbabwean Diaspora — a primary target market with significant USD foreign currency investment potential and a demonstrated appetite for quality housing.
