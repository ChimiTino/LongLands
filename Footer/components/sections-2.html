Why Invest
in Longlands?

Proven Market Performance
Gated communities command higher prices per m². Graylands Park, a comparable Harare South estate, launched at US$40/m² and now trades at US$72/m² — an 80% appreciation that underscores the sector's strength.
Economies of Scale
Nissam supplies its own quarry products, sand, bricks, and concrete blocks — dramatically reducing construction costs and passing premium value directly to investors and homeowners.
Asset-Backed Bond Innovation
Nissam is pioneering a tradeable Asset-Backed Bond secured against receivables, likely in partnership with a listed REIT — offering investors stability, liquidity, and currency risk mitigation.
Diaspora Target Market
Longlands Park is designed to be exceptionally appealing to the Zimbabwean Diaspora community — a primary target market with significant USD foreign currency investment potential and demonstrated appetite for quality housing.
